## 🚀 Getting Started

### Initial Setup
1. Download the app from Garmin Connect IQ Store
2. Sync with your Garmin device
3. Open the app on your device
4. Configure your settings on first launch

### Recommended Initial Settings
- **Target Duration**: 5 minutes (for beginners)
- **Max Heart Rate**: Based on age (220 - age)
- **Min Heart Rate**: 40 bpm
- **Min Body Temperature**: 35°C
- **Alerts**: On

## 🏊 Using the Activity

### Preparation
1. **Warm-up**: 5-10 minutes of light exercise
2. **Breathing Work**: Deep breathing exercises
3. **Mental Preparation**: Stay calm and focused

### Starting the Activity
```
1. Open the app
2. Wait for "Ready" message
3. Press SELECT button
4. Green recording indicator will appear
5. Enter cold water
```

### During Activity
- **Screen Indicators**:
  - Top: Duration (large font)
  - Middle: Heart rate ❤️
  - Lower: Body temperature 🌡️
  - Bottom: Calories 🔥

- **Color Alerts**:
  - 🟢 Green dot: Recording active
  - 🔴 Red banner: Warning present
  - ⚠️ Yellow: Caution needed

### Ending the Activity
```
1. Press BACK button
2. Select from menu:
   - "Save" → Save activity
   - "Discard" → Delete activity
   - "Resume" → Return to activity
```

## 🛡️ Safety Guide

### Critical Warnings

#### 🔴 High Heart Rate (>160 bpm)
**What to Do:**
- Slow down your breathing
- Try to stay calm
- Exit water if necessary

#### 💙 Low Heart Rate (<40 bpm)
**What to Do:**
- **EXIT IMMEDIATELY!**
- Warm your body
- Seek medical help if needed

#### 🌡️ Low Body Temperature (<35°C)
**What to Do:**
- **EXIT WATER IMMEDIATELY!**
- Put on dry clothes
- Drink warm (not hot!) beverages
- Seek medical attention

#### ⏰ Time Limit Reached
**What to Do:**
- End the activity
- Exit water slowly
- Begin warming routine

### Safety Tips
✅ **DO:**
- Never go alone
- Have a friend or supervisor present
- Keep your phone nearby
- Progress slowly
- Listen to your body
- Practice regularly (for adaptation)

❌ **DON'T:**
- Enter while intoxicated
- Enter when sick or tired
- Push beyond your limits
- Make sudden movements
- Ignore warnings

## ⚙️ Settings

### Target Duration
- **Beginners**: 2-5 minutes
- **Intermediate**: 5-10 minutes
- **Advanced**: 10-15 minutes
- **Expert**: 15+ minutes

### Heart Rate Thresholds
**Maximum Heart Rate Calculation:**
```
Max HR = 220 - Your Age
Example: 30 years → 220 - 30 = 190 bpm
Recommended threshold: 80-85% → 152-162 bpm
```

**Minimum Heart Rate:**
- Generally 40 bpm is safe
- May be lower for athletes
- Consult your doctor

### Temperature Settings
- **Celsius**: European standard
- **Fahrenheit**: US standard
- **Hypothermia Threshold**: 35°C (95°F)

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Body Temperature Not Showing
**Solution:**
1. Check if your device supports body temperature sensor
2. Wear watch tightly on wrist
3. Wait a few minutes (sensor warm-up time)

### Incorrect Heart Rate
**Solution:**
1. Wear watch tighter
2. Warm up your wrists
3. Stay still (during measurement)
4. Try using a chest strap

### Alerts Not Working
**Solution:**
1. Check Settings → "Enable Alerts"
2. Check device volume
3. Check vibration settings

### Activity Not Saving
**Solution:**
1. Check for GPS signal
2. Ensure device has enough storage
3. Sync with Garmin Connect

## 💡 Tips and Tricks

### For Better Experience
1. **Be Consistent**: 3-4 times per week
2. **Progress Slowly**: Add 30 seconds each week
3. **Breathing Work**: Wim Hof method
4. **Warming Routine**: Important after activity
5. **Nutrition**: Light meal before

### Cold Adaptation
- First 2 weeks: 2-3 minutes
- Weeks 3-4: 5 minutes
- Weeks 5-8: 8-10 minutes
- Week 8+: 10-15 minutes
